Monitor Your Accounts Regularly

Regular account monitoring helps identify suspicious activity early.

5. Monitor Your Accounts Regularly

Why Monitoring Matters

Checking your financial transactions regularly helps identify unauthorised purchases, suspicious transfers, or unusual account activity.

What to Check

  • Unexpected withdrawals
  • Unknown purchases
  • Changes to account settings
  • Unrecognised login attempts

Best Practice

Enable transaction notifications through your banking app to receive instant updates about account activity.
