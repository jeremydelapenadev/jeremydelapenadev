What is Phishing?

Phishing scams attempt to trick users into providing sensitive information by pretending to be trusted organisations, such as banks.

Warning Signs

  • Unexpected emails asking for urgent action
  • Misspelled email addresses
  • Suspicious attachments
  • Requests for banking information
  • Unsecured websites (http:// instead of https://)

How to Stay Safe

Never click suspicious links. Instead, log in directly through your bank's official website or mobile app.
