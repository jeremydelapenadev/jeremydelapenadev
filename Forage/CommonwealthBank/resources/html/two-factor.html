What is Two-Factor Authentication?

Two-factor authentication (2FA) provides additional protection by requiring two methods of verification before access is granted to an account.

Examples of Verification

  • Password or PIN
  • One-time SMS code
  • Authentication app approval
  • Biometric login (Face ID or fingerprint)

Why It Matters

Even if a cybercriminal obtains your password, 2FA makes it significantly harder to gain access to your bank account.
