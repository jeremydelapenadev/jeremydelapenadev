Protect your financial information by using strong and unique passwords.

Why Strong Passwords Matter

Weak passwords are one of the most common ways hackers gain access to bank accounts and personal information. Cybercriminals use automated software to guess passwords quickly, especially when they contain birthdays, names, or common words.

How to Create a Strong Password

  • Use at least 12 to 16 characters
  • Include uppercase and lowercase letters
  • Add numbers and special symbols
  • Avoid personal information
  • Never reuse passwords across accounts

Recommended Practice

Consider using a password manager to safely generate and store secure, unique passwords for financial accounts.
